The Computer Science Society

The Computer Science Society strives to extend beyond the conventional domains of computing through a series of multi-disciplinary events. Most importantly, we wish to familiarize people with technology, to increase digital literacy through fun events, talks, meme competitions, blogs, journal, and Instagram posts. We are dedicated to the promotion of the various facets of computing. We strive to promote gaming, technology, applications, programming, science, and fiction, inter alia. We are a melange of people brought together by a common idea: Tech is the future.

About Us

The Computer Science Society is just a group of students who wonder what the epithet we are named after means - from the hardware to the software. We have pledged ( and signed digitally - once on the internet it remains on the internet ) to foster interest and creative expression of computational ideas of various forms - and making the process of learning an interesting and fun one.

Whether it’s questions like - ‘ Why is RTX 3090 so expensive ?’ or ‘ Can you hear me ?’ over Google Meet or ‘ A system like a Matrix - will it ever be possible ?’ We do our best to answer them and debunk those pesky myths - just by participating in our fun events. From music or gaming sessions to updates on technological advancements, learning about programming languages - an array of events for everyone. Or is quizzing your foray? maybe writing, watching movies, or being ambitious and how to use computer science for your favor - your every niche idea is fulfilled here.

And whether it is just one click of a mouse or a touch of a screen - gives thousands of possibilities but how? How all these bits are interconnected to give ‘life’ to a machine we carry around. And just like us they think, grow and die off - and we are here to thread between those lines. At the end of the day when the screens go black and the machines are put to recharge - we hope our events give people a chance to interact, learn, solve puzzles, grow and most of all have fun while doing it.

About Us

The Computer Science Society, a.k.a. CompSoc. We are a bunch of geeks set out to make the ever-evolving world of Information Technology more enjoyable, and understandable for everyone. The society began in 1991 when a group of students from the College came together to share their love for technology with the world. It has been our agenda to profess that computing is beyond writing complex code.
